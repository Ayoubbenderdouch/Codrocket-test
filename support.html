üìö Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Codrocket app from the App Store, open it, and tap "Register". Fill in your business details including name, email, phone number, and create a secure password. You'll need a valid Algerian phone number for registration.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most products have a minimum order quantity of 10 pieces. This is standard for B2B wholesale purchasing. Specific minimum quantities are displayed on each product page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • CCP (Algeria)
  • Baridimob
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash on Delivery
  • PayPal
  • Binance (Cryptocurrency)
  • EU Bank Transfer

Which currencies are supported?

Codrocket supports three currencies: DZD (Algerian Dinar), USD (US Dollar), and EUR (Euro). You can select your preferred currency when placing custom orders.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We offer international shipping with two options: Standard Shipping and DDP (Delivered Duty Paid). Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

What is a Custom Order?

If you can't find a product in our catalog, you can create a custom order request. Simply provide the product details, description, URL (if available), quantity needed, and delivery information. Our team will review and get back to you with a quote.

How do I track my order?

Go to the "Orders" tab in the app to view your complete order history. Each order shows its current status: Pending, Confirmed, Shipped, or Delivered. You'll receive notifications as your order status updates.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified only if they haven't been confirmed yet. Contact our support team immediately at support@sourci.com with your order number if you need to make changes.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and secure authentication (Laravel Sanctum) to protect all your data. Payment information is encrypted and never stored on your device.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Can I use the app without logging in?

Yes, you can browse products without an account. However, to place orders and track your purchase history, you'll need to create an account and log in.

What categories of products do you offer?

We offer products in multiple categories:

  • Electronics & Computers
  • Office Furniture & Equipment
  • Stationery & Office Supplies
  • Food & Beverages
  • Cleaning Products
  • Medical Equipment

üîß Technical Support

The app won't load products

Try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • If the problem persists, contact support

I can't log in

Ensure you're using the correct email/phone and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team.

App crashes or freezes

Make sure you have the latest version of the app and iOS. Restart your device and try again. If the issue continues, contact support with details about when the crash occurs.
